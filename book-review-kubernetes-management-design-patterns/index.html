Book review: Kubernetes management design patterns

23 September 2017

Writing about an evolving piece of software such as Kubernetes must be quite of a task, and rarely gets done right.

Unfortunately, “Kubernetes management design patterns” is kind of a boring version of the official docs, and won’t offer much to k8s’ aficionados, who already understand a bunch of patterns around k8s deployments.

I really don’t want to sound too negative, this was probably a bad buy from my side — I didn’t even get to the end of the book as I didn’t find it interesting.
